AKAN NAMES

It's unique and interesting how the Akan people of Ghana name their children. They name them after the day of the week they were born. Yes! Imagine being named Wednesday or Sunday. Funny, right? This is the culture that the people of Ghana have become accustomed to since way back when. Come along and discover, not only the day of the week you were born, but also your Akan name.
